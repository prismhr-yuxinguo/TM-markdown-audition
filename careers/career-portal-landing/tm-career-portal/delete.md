<h3>Frequently Asked Questions:</h3>





<ul class="list">
    <li><em>How long does the process typically take?</em> Our recruitment process can take anywhere
        from 2-3 weeks,
        dependent of course, on the role.</li>
    <li><em>I see there are several openings on the career page, do I need to apply to all of them to
            maximize my
            chances of selection?</em> You are encouraged to apply to roles that closely match your
        skill set and
        experience.</h3>
        <p>
    </li>
    <li><em>Will interviews be conducted virtually or in person? </em>The majority of our interviews are
        being
        conducted virtually. However if you are close to one of our local offices, or you are based in
        India, we
        will schedule an in person interview for select roles.</h3>
        <p>
    </li>
    <li><em>How should I prepare for my first screening with a Talent Acquisition Partner?</em> The
        first stage of
        our process is a screen by one of our Talent Acquisition Partners or Talent Acquisition
        Sourcers. This
        screening will cover your work history and particulars like compensation, benefits and other
        details.</h3>
        <p>
    </li>
    <li><em>Will you contact my current employer as part of the recruiting process? </em>We will not
        contact any
        current or previous employer without permission from you after our process is completed, usually
        at the
        reference checking stage and background check stage.</h3>
        <p>
    </li>
    <li><em>What does career development look like at PrismHR? </em>We realize that there is more to
        work than just
        your actual job and we offer continuous learning and development opportunities both inside and
        outside our
        walls.</h3>
        <p>
    </li>
</ul>If you are unable to find an opening that fits your particular skill set or experience, please
apply to our
Future Consideration role and we will retain your application for a potential future
opening.</h3>
